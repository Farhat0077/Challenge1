Skills needed for full-stack development:
1. Proficiency in frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
2. Knowledge of backend technologies such as Node.js, Python, Ruby on Rails, or Java.
3. Understanding of databases (SQL and NoSQL), web servers, and APIs.
4. Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git) and deployment processes.
5. Problem-solving abilities and debugging skills across the full stack.
